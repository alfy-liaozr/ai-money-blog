## How to Choose the Right Robot Vacuum

Consider these factors:

1. **Flooring:** Carpets need stronger suction (Roomba j9+); hard floors benefit from mopping (Roborock S8).
2. **Budget:** Basic models ($150-300) vs. premium ($800-1400).
3. **Smart features:** LiDAR navigation vs. random bump; app control vs. remote.
4. **Maintenance:** Self-emptying bins = less frequent emptying.
5. **Pet hair:** Look for tangle-free brush rolls.
