## How to Choose the Right Laptop for School

Consider these factors:

1. **Budget:** How much can you afford? Don't overspend if you don't need high-end specs.
2. **Program requirements:** Engineering/CS needs more power than English Lit.
3. **Portability:** Will you carry it all day? Go for under 3 lbs.
4. **Battery life:** Look for 8+ hours for all-day classes.
5. **Operating System:** macOS vs Windows vs ChromeOS - choose what your school uses.
