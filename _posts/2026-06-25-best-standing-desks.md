## Pro Tips for Standing Desks

- **Use an anti-fatigue mat** — your feet will thank you
- **Alternate sitting and standing** every 45-60 minutes
- **Start with 30 minutes per day** and gradually increase
- **Position your monitor at eye level** to avoid neck strain
