## How to Choose the Right Wireless Earbuds

Consider these factors:
1. **Ecosystem:** iPhone users should consider AirPods; Android users have more options
2. **ANC:** Active Noise Cancellation is essential for commuting/travel
3. **Battery life:** Look for 6+ hours per charge (24+ with case)
4. **Water resistance:** IPX4 or higher for workouts
5. **Fit:** Try before buying if possible; comfort is crucial
