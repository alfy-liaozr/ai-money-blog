## How to Choose the Right Dog Toy

When selecting a toy for your dog, consider:
1. **Size:** Large dogs need larger, more durable toys
2. **Chewing style:** Aggressive chewers need tough rubber or nylon toys
3. **Play preferences:** Does your dog prefer fetch, tug, or puzzle toys?
4. **Safety:** Avoid toys with small parts that could be swallowed
