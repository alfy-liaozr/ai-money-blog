## How to Choose the Right Coffee Maker

Consider these factors:

1. **Brew style:** Drip, espresso, pour-over, single-serve, or cold brew?
2. **Budget:** $30 (AeroPress) to $750 (Breville)
3. **Convenience:** Nespresso/Keurig (instant) vs. pour-over (manual)
4. **Capacity:** Single-serve vs. 8-12 cup carafes
5. **Maintenance:** Some require descaling, others are dishwasher-safe
