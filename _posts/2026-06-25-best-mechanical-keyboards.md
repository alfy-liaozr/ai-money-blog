## Key Terms Explained

| Term | What It Means |
|------|--------------|
| **Form Factor** | Size of keyboard: Full (num pad), TKL (no num pad), 75%, 60% |
| **Switch Type** | Linear (smooth), Tactile (bump), Clicky (bump + sound) |
| **Hot-swappable** | You can change switches without soldering |
| **PBT Keycaps** | More durable than ABS, won't get shiny over time |
| **QMK/VIA** | Open-source firmware for unlimited customization |
