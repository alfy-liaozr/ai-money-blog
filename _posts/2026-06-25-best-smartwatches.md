## How to Choose the Right Smartwatch

Consider these factors:

1. **Ecosystem:** Apple Watch for iOS, Galaxy Watch for Android
2. **Battery life:** Garmin/Fitbit (weeks) vs. Apple/Samsung (1-2 days)
3. **Sports features:** Garmin for serious athletes, Apple for general fitness
4. **Budget:** $200 (Amazfit) to $800 (Apple Ultra)
5. **Health sensors:** ECG, blood oxygen, body composition
